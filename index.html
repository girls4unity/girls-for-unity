Who Are We

Welcome to Girls for Unity, a coalition of like-minded individuals dedicated to empowering young girls of color and igniting a passion for social justice.

We are a coalition of writers and likeminded change makers who aim to make an impact on young minds. We hope to be leaders of reform and to inspire young girls to harbor a passion for justice as well.
